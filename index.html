Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the image descriptions?
Our AI model has been trained on millions of images and can provide highly accurate descriptions for most common images. It works best with clear photographs, illustrations, and diagrams. For very specialized or technical images, the descriptions may be more general.
Does the extension work on all websites?
Yes, the extension works on most websites. However, some websites with strict content security policies may block the extension from accessing images. In these cases, you can download the image and use the extension's upload feature.
Is my data private and secure?
Yes, we take privacy very seriously. Images are only temporarily processed to generate descriptions and are not stored on our servers. We do not collect any personal information beyond what's necessary for the extension to function. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
Can I use the descriptions commercially?
Yes, all descriptions generated by the extension are yours to use however you like, including for commercial purposes. However, please note that the extension does not transfer any copyright of the original images.
How do I cancel my premium subscription?
You can cancel your premium subscription at any time from the extension's settings page. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
